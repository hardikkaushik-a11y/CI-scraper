Actian Competitive Intelligence
Dashboard Guide
This dashboard monitors — competitor companies' job postings in real-time to surface competitive hiring intelligence for Actian's strategy and product teams.
How It Works
🔍
Scraper — Pulls fresh job postings daily from competitor career pages via Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, Workable APIs and direct HTML scraping.
🧠
Enricher — Claude AI classifies each job's Function, Product Focus, Seniority, and Skills, then scores it for relevancy to Actian.
📊
Dashboard — This view. Interactive charts, filters, and AI-generated strategic signals to help Actian make informed competitive decisions.
Dashboard Pages
Market Overview — KPIs, interactive charts (click bars to filter), and a full job table with 7 filter dimensions. Use this to explore hiring patterns across companies.
Strategic Signals — Claude-generated analysis per company: threat level, implications, and recommended actions. Use this for executive briefings and strategy discussions.
Key Metrics Explained
Relevancy Score (0–10)Rating scale: How strategically important a job is to Actian's business. We factor in: the number of skills that match Actian's tech stack (SQL, ETL, AWS, Kubernetes, etc.), whether the role is in a high-priority product area for us (Data Governance, Streaming, Vector DBs), the seniority level (Director-level hires are more strategic), geographic location (US/EU preferred), and whether there's AI/ML involved.
8–10: High-signal, move fast
5–7: Medium signal, worth monitoring
Below 5: Lower priority
Threat LevelCRITICAL / HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW. AI-assessed risk from each company based on hiring volume, seniority mix, product focus, and competitive proximity.
Trend Score1 = this role is trending (recently posted or high-signal job title). 0 = steady-state.
Product FocusAI-classified product area the job is hiring for — e.g. ETL/Integration, ML/AI infra, Data Governance.
New This WeekRoles posted within 7 days. High spikes = active hiring push.
Tips for Effective Use
  • Click any chart bar to instantly filter the job table to that segment.
  • Min Relevancy slider — drag to 8+ to see only high-signal jobs.
  • Signals page — click a company card to expand full AI analysis.
  • Ask the AI assistant (bottom-right button) to query any trend or pattern in natural language.
  • Data refreshes daily. "Days Since Posted" counts from the original posting date.
Data sources: Career pages of — competitor companies scraped via their ATS APIs (Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, Workable) plus Playwright for JS-heavy sites. 365-day rolling window. Legal and People/HR roles excluded.
